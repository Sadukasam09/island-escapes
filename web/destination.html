Colombo

Discover the vibrant capital city with its rich history and modern attractions.

Colombo is a bustling metropolis that blends colonial heritage with contemporary culture. Explore its lively markets, beautiful parks, and the stunning waterfront.

Kandy

Experience the cultural heart of Sri Lanka and visit the Temple of the Tooth.

Kandy is surrounded by lush hills and is famous for its sacred Buddhist sites. The Kandy Lake and the vibrant local markets add to its charm.

Galle

Explore the historic Galle Fort and enjoy the beautiful coastal views.

Galle is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its well-preserved colonial architecture. Stroll through its narrow streets and enjoy the stunning sunsets by the sea.

Nuwara Eliya

Visit the picturesque tea plantations and enjoy the cool climate.

Often referred to as "Little England," Nuwara Eliya features beautiful gardens, tea estates, and a charming colonial atmosphere amidst the hills.

Sigiriya

Climb the iconic Lion Rock and explore ancient ruins.

Sigiriya is a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its rock fortress and frescoes. The views from the top are simply breathtaking.

Anuradhapura

Discover the ancient city and its remarkable archaeological sites.

Anuradhapura was one of the ancient capitals of Sri Lanka and is home to many well-preserved ruins of ancient Sri Lankan civilization.

Hikkaduwa

Relax on beautiful beaches and enjoy vibrant marine life.

Hikkaduwa is famous for its stunning beaches, coral reefs, and water sports. It's a great spot for snorkeling, diving, and enjoying the lively beach atmosphere.

Trincomalee

Discover pristine beaches and historical sites.

Trincomalee is known for its beautiful beaches, such as Uppuveli and Nilaveli, as well as its rich history, including the famous Koneswaram Temple.

Mirissa

Enjoy whale watching and stunning sunsets.

Mirissa is a small beach town famous for its whale watching tours and beautiful beaches. It's a perfect place to relax and enjoy the sunset.

Yala National Park

Experience wildlife safaris and stunning landscapes.

Yala National Park is home to a diverse range of wildlife, including leopards, elephants, and a variety of bird species. It's a must-visit for nature and animal lovers.

Kithulgala

Adventure awaits with white-water rafting and trekking.

Kithulgala is known for its adventure sports, including white-water rafting and trekking. The beautiful scenery along the Kelani River makes it a great destination for thrill-seekers.

Jaffna

Explore the unique culture and heritage of Northern Sri Lanka.

Jaffna offers a different perspective of Sri Lanka with its rich Tamil culture, ancient temples, and delicious cuisine. Visit the Jaffna Fort and nearby islands for a unique experience.

Polonnaruwa

Explore the medieval capital and its impressive monuments.

Polonnaruwa is known for its ancient temples and statues, showcasing the grandeur of Sri Lanka's medieval period.
